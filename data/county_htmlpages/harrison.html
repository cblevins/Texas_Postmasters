*MORTUARY.

B. E. ETHERIDGE.

Hallsville, Tex., June 24. -- Major B. E. Etheridge, aged 64, a merchant and postmaster here for many years, and a brother to Hon. Emmerson Etheridge, ex-collector of the port at Memphis, Tenn., died, and was buried by the Masonic order to-day.

- June 25, 1895, The Dallas Morning News, p. 2.
