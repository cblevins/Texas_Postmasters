To Postmasters & Post Offices of Nacogdoches County, Texas

LOONEYVILLE.  A postoffice in Nacogdoches
county, 18 miles from Nacogdoches, the judicial
seat, nearest banking and shipping point.  Population,
100.  Mail, tri-weekly.  George W. Bush, postmaster.

Baxter, R. M., corn mill and gin.
Bush, George W., Physician.
Rayney, N. L., justice of peace.

______

Source: Polk's Texas State Gazeteer & Business Directory, 1890-1891, p. 715.
