To Postmasters & Post Offices of Washington County, Texas

LOTT. A postoffice in Washington
county, 20 miles northeast of Brenham,
the judicial seat, and 12 miles southwest
of Navasota, the nearest banking and
shipping point. Population, 200.  
J. B. Lott, postmaster.
 
Wells, O., cotton gin.
 
 
Source: Polk's Texas State Gazeteer & Business Directory,
1890-1891, p. 715.
